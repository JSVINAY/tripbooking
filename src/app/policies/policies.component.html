Privacy Policy

Welcome to HolidayMakers! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy outlines how we handle your information:

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email, phone number, and address when you sign up or book trips.
  • Payment details (processed securely) for booking transactions.
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information.

2. Use of Information

  • To process bookings and provide customer support.
  • To improve our website and services based on user behavior.
  • To send promotional offers, if you have opted in for marketing communications.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. However, we may share it with trusted third parties for:

  • Processing payments securely.
  • Providing travel-related services like flights and hotels.
  • Compliance with legal obligations.

4. Your Rights

  • Access and update your personal data through your account settings.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal or contractual obligations.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.

5. Security Measures

We implement robust security measures to protect your data, including encryption and regular security audits.

6. Updates to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be communicated via email or on our website.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at gifts@holidaymakers.com
